                          SWADHARMA SETU 

1. Project Title
Swadharma Setu 
A digital platform designed to revive, preserve, and teach Hindu Sanathana Dharma through modern digital learning.

3. Introduction
Exploring the Hindu Sanathana Dharma Culture to the present generation from childrens to senior citizens through digital learning .In today’s generation even the educated people are also disconnected to the our culture.So from this app we will get connected to our culture.

4. Objectives
 Spread awareness of Sanathana Dharma.
 Provide cultural, spiritual, and historical education.
 Offer slokas, stories, videos, and simplified content.
 Create childfriendly learning modules.
 Build partnerships with temples and spiritual organizations.
 Offer dharmic product marketplace and delivery partner integration.

5. Target Audience
 Childrens
 Schools and cultural organizations
 Parents and families
 Cultural learners and devotees
